The Malayan Tiger

About

The Malayan tiger is a tiger from a specific population of the Panthera tigris tigris subspecies that is native to Peninsular Malaysia. This population inhabits the southern and central parts of the Malay Peninsula, and has been classified as critically endangered. Wikipedia

Scientific name: Panthera tigris jacksoni

Class: Mammalia

Domain: Eukaryota

Family: Felidae

Genus: Panthera

Kingdom: Animalia

Order: Carnivora
