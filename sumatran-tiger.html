The Sumatran tiger

About

The Sumatran tiger is a population of Panthera tigris sondaica on the Indonesian island of Sumatra. It is the only surviving tiger population in the Sunda Islands, where the Bali and Javan tigers are extinct. Wikipedia

Conservation status: Critically Endangered (Population decreasing) Encyclopedia of Life

Scientific name: Panthera tigris sumatrae

Higher classification: Tiger
