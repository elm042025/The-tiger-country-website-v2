The Siberian tiger

About

The Siberian tiger or Amur tiger is a population of the tiger subspecies Panthera tigris tigris native to Northeast China, the Russian Far East, and possibly North Korea.Wikipedia 

Conservation status: Endangered (Population stable)Encyclopedia of Life 

Scientific name: Panthera tigris altaica

Family: Felidae

Class: Mammalia

Domain: Eukaryota

Genus: Panthera

Kingdom: Animalia
