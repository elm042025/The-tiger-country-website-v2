The Indochinese Tiger

About

The Indochinese tiger is a population of the Panthera tigris tigris subspecies that is native to Southeast Asia. This population occurs in Myanmar and Thailand. Wikipedia

Conservation status: Endangered (Population decreasing)Encyclopedia of Life

Scientific name: Panthera tigris corbetti

Family: Felidae

Class: Mammalia

Genus: Panthera

Kingdom: Animalia

Order: Carnivora
